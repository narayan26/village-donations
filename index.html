Featured Village: Kunnathur Colony

Kunnathur Colony, located in the Sholingur Block of Ranipet District, is 36 kilometers from the district headquarters. The village is home to approximately 350 families, predominantly belonging to the Scheduled Caste Adidravidar community.

The majority of men in the village are engaged in Pattarai work (traditionally associated with weaving or manual labor), while most women participate in the MGNREGS (100-day employment scheme) for their livelihood. Additionally, some men and women work as agricultural laborers on farmlands owned by higher-caste landowners.

In this colony, only a middle school is available. Students in 9th and 10th grades must travel 5 kilometers to attend the nearest higher secondary school, while those in 11th and 12th grades need to travel 6 kilometers to Narasingapuram.
